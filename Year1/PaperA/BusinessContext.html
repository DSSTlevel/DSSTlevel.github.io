Organisation Types

Public - Owned/operated by government agencies.
Private - Owned by individuals and often made to make profit
  • Small or Medium-sized enterprise (SME)
  • Large enterprise
  • Non-governmental Organisation (NGO)
    • Voluntary - Non-profit Organisation. benefit society (National Trust)
    Often make money through gift shops to re-invest back in (direct contribution)

    Stakeholder Types

    Internal

    Owners - Direct the business, control the day-to-day operations and processes
    Board of Directors - Elected to represent shareholders' interests
    Employees - Have an employment contract. Can be employed but a different tax. Classed as workers
    Departments - Organised by their roles. Typical businesses tend to have the following:
    Financial, Admin, HR, Research and Development, Sales, Customer Service

    External

    Customers/Consumers - The end user that is purchasing goods
    Direct/Indirect competitors - Other businesses that are in competition with the business selling that product/service
    Shareholders - People that have an interest of the business and have a say how money in spent
    Investors - A person who inputs money into a business for a financial return
    Funders - Support organisations that have a large varty of shareholders

    Factors

    Political

    These are factors that impact how the business operates. Often from the government.
    Such as tax and foreign trade policies, agendas.

    Economical

    These are factors that impact a business' money.
    Such as Interest/Exchange rates, recession, trends.

    Social

    These are factors that impact a business' customers and employees.
    Such as horizontal mobility, vertical mobility.

    Technological

    These are factors that use emerging technologies to try and reduce operational costs.
    Such as automation, increasing revenue.

    Legal

    These are factors that impact a business' operations and abilities.
    Such as laws and regulations. You can view them here

    Environmental

    These factors that impact the business' environment and area.
    Such as Carbon footprint, Digital waste.
