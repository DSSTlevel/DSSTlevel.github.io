Different Cables and Wires

Connectors

A 20/24-pin slotted connector

Connects to the motherboard

SATA Keys Connector

Connects disk drives

Connector is wider and thinner than Molex

Molex Keyed Connector

Connects hard drives, optical drives, or other devices

Berg Keyed Connector

Connect to legacy floppy drives

Smaller than a Molex connector

4/8pin auxillary power connector

Connector has two to 8-pin and supplies power to different areas of the motherboard

Smaller but same shape as main connector

6/8pin PCIe power connector

Two rows of three to four pins

Supplies power to internal components

Video Ports and Cables

Digital Visual Interface (DVI)

Connector commonly white, made of 24pins for digital signals, 4 pins for analog signals, flat pin called a ground bar.

Display Port

Interface technology

Designed to connect high-end graphics-capable PC's and displays. Additionally, home theatre equipment

High-Definition Multimedia Interface (HDMI)

Developed for high-definition TV's. digital features create good candidate for computers

Thunderbolt 1/2

High-speed connection of peripherals such as hard drives

Transmit high-definition video using the DisplayPort protocol

Thunderbolt 3

Uses same USB-C connector

x2 bandwith of T2, less power, provide 2 4K monitors with video

Video Graphics Array (VGA)

Connector for analog video

3rows of 15pins. Often called: DE-15 or HD-15

Radio Corporation of America (RCA)

Central plug with a ring around it. Used to carry audio or video

Often in 3 plugs. Yellow-video. red+white-left/right audio channels

Other Ports and Cables

Personal System 2 (PS/2)

Connects a keyboard/mouse to a computer. Often colored differently. 6pin mini-DIN female connector

Audio and Game Port

Audio ports connect audio devices. Analog - Connect to an external source. Mic port and line out ports connect speaakers.

Game port connects a joystick or MIDI device

Network Port

Also called RJ-45, 8pins and connects devices to a network. Connection speed depends on the network port type. Max length is 100m

Serial AT Attachment (SATA)

Connects SATA devices to SATA interface. 7pin

L-Shaped Slot. Cable supplies NO power.

Integrated Drive Electronics (IDE)

Ribbon cable used to connect storage drives inside the computer

Most common: 34-pin cable for floppy drives, 40-pin for HDD and Optical Drives

Universal Serial Bus (USB)

Connects peripheral devices to a computer. Can disconnect and connect the devices while the computer is still powered
