Top Tips!

Here is a list of top tips to bring to Alton Towers tomorrow! (Especially for the ones who are new to going)
  1. Travel Lightly – Bring as less as you can on the trip so you arent carrying a lot around with out
  2. Bring atleast £20 with you for food. It is quite expensive!
  3. Nemesis Is CLOSED – Which means you will see a construction site, however Galactica is still open!
  4. You can view live queue times at Queue-times.org
  5. Wear something comfortable, there is a LOT of hills!
  6. Try and skip out the Gardens if you can, that’s if the skyride isn’t open
  7. Try and avoid the wettest rides until the end of the day, you don’t want to be walking around wet. Unless it is hot weather
  8. Peak times for restaurants and catering are between 11am-1pm
  9. Queue times are going to be quite big as this is the school season, so prepare for waiting a long time
  10. I’d recommend doing X-Sector first as that’s where everyone flocks to, this is at the back of the park, if you turn right at the entrance and go straight down the long path, you can see it from there (Just to the right of the towers (the big castle that people call it))
  11. Go to the gift shop last, last thing you want to do is carry around an item that could break or you could lose whilst your trip there
  12. Everyone starts to leave at around 3, so get them big rides in!
  13. Bring some sort of charging battery, youll most likely be using your phone most of the day, as theme parks are going to digital with their systems
